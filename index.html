Invitation Management Hub

The all-in-one solution for creating, managing, and tracking your event invitations with futuristic flair.

1. Simple Editor

Visually craft your invitation. Upload a base design and interactively place guest details.

2. Manage Guests

Generate unique, personalized invitation links and monitor guest RSVP status in real-time.

3. Guest Check-in

Streamline event entry. Use any device camera to scan guest QR codes for instant check-in.

4. Template Composer (Advanced)

Unleash your creativity. Build fully custom templates from scratch with a Canva-like editor. Add multiple texts, logos, and QR codes anywhere on the canvas.
